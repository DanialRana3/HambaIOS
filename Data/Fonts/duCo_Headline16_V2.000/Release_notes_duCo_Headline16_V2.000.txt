==Release notes_du Co Headline 16==

Compiler of release notes: Dalton Maag
Designer: Dalton Maag
Designer URL: daltonmaag.com

Date of creation: 2014-07-21

Changes since previous release:
- Added cyrillic and greek scripts.
- Changed embedding settings to Installable.

Basic information 
1 Basic Family name: du Co Headline 16
2 TrueType Version record: 2.000
3 Supported scripts : Latin, Arabic, Greek, Cyrillic
4 Number of glyphs: 1148
5 Weights/Styles: Light, Regular, Bold

Technology 
1 Files formats: The files have been delivered in the TrueType-based OpenType format, for use in all major Operating Systems, and in web font format: EOT; WOFF; WOFF2.
2 Embedding settings: Everything is allowed (installable mode); subsetting is allowed.
3 Hinting information: All glyphs are manually hinted for optimized rasterization in Microsoft ClearType or for other LCD devices.
4 Default numerals: Proportional numerals as default.
